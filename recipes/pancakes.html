Pancake Recipe

Description

To make the perfect fluffy pancakes you will need the following ingredients:

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Baking Powder
  • Salt
  • Butter/Cooking oil

Steps

  1. First step is to place your flour, salt, sugar and baking powder into a mixing bowl. Make a small crater in the middle.
  2. Crack an egg or two into the crater you made previously. Gently mix the egg into the mixture.
  3. Pour your milk into the mixture and wisk till smooth.
  4. Heat up a pan on the stove and place either a small bit of butter or oil onto the pan.
  5. Use a ladle to scoop pancake mixture into the pan and cook till golden on both sides.
